# 4-Week Email Warmup Schedule for logicalfront.net

This document outlines the day-by-day sending schedule to warm up the new domain `logicalfront.net` on Amazon SES over a 28-day period. The goal is to gradually increase sending volume to establish a positive sender reputation with Internet Service Providers (ISPs).

## Audience Segments

The warmup process will prioritize the most engaged users first to build a strong initial reputation.

- **Segment A: Highly Engaged:** Users who have opened or clicked an email in the last 30 days.
- **Segment B: Moderately Engaged:** Users who have opened or clicked an email in the last 31-90 days.
- **Segment C: Less Engaged:** Users who have opened an email more than 90 days ago, or new subscribers with validated emails.
